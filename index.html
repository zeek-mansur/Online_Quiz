Welcome to Online Quiz

Instrcutions
  • The quiz comprises of 10 Questions with total countdown time of 400 seconds
  • Once the start button is clicked the timer starts running
  • Quiz must be completed before the timer runs down
  • Once answer is selected it can not be changed
  • Click the next button on the question page 'only' after question has been answered
  • Result is displayed after all questions have been answered and you can restart the quiz
  • Click 'Start Quiz' to get started

Features :-

  • 10 Questions
  • 400 seconds total timer
  • Random Question
  • Display final results
